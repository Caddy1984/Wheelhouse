Our Story

Established in 1966 by our grandfather - we are a family run business situated in Acton, London. Gramps was inspired by his love of coffee to share his favourite brew with this wonderful community that he loved so much.

Our Values

Our coffee is organic. Our packaging is sustainable (no plastics here!), and our food is sourced from local suppliers. A percentage of all sales are donated to environmental charities. Ask us for more information.

Our Vision

We honour Gramps' legacy by delivering fresh organic coffee to the community in a friendly and environmentally concious manner. We envision a happy community, enjoying great coffee and living in a cleaner, greener London
